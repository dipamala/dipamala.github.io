Sativa-Dominant & Hybrid

The Wedding Cake strain is distinguishable by its large, vibrant flowers. Its buds are dense and bulbous, typical of indica strains, and are adorned by bright orange pistils and tightly curled leaves.

The high concentration of trichomes found on the buds are responsible for this strain's high THC levels and sticky resin texture. The name of this strain can be attributed to this sparkling resin coat, which gives off the appearance of vanilla wedding cake frosting. This strain also goes by the alias 'Pink Cookies' in reference to the pink and purple hues that highlight its buds.
